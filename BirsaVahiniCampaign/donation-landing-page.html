Centuries of History, Weathered by Time's Neglect

Deep within Mahadevtoli village lie the silent remnants of a once-thriving temple. These sacred stones, carved by our ancestors over 3,000 years ago, have withstood the test of time. Today, this holy site is neglected, its priceless artifacts scattered and unprotected.

Without our immediate intervention, this invaluable link to our heritage could be lost forever. Birsa Vahini Foundation has pledged to protect and restore this site, and we need your support to make it happen.

Our Phased Restoration Plan

Site Reclamation & Protection

Our first step is to secure the area to prevent further damage, clear overgrowth, and carefully catalog every artifact.

Archaeological Assessment

We will work with archaeologists and historians to study the site and ensure the restoration is historically accurate and respectful.

Restoration & Rebuilding

Under expert supervision, skilled artisans will begin the painstaking process of cleaning, repairing, and reassembling the temple structure to restore its sanctity.
